About MoneyMate

MoneyMate is a smart expense tracking web application designed to help users manage their income and spending efficiently. It enables users to add, track, and visualize their daily, weekly, and monthly transactions through dynamic charts and a simple dashboard. With secure login and OTP-based editing, MoneyMate ensures both convenience and privacy.
